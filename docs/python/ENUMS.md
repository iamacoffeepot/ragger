## Enums (`src/ragger/enums.py`)

- `Skill(IntEnum)` ‚Äî 23 OSRS skills, int-based with `label`, `mask` properties
- `Region(IntEnum)` ‚Äî 12 regions (including GENERAL), int-based with `label`, `mask`, `from_label` properties
- `TaskDifficulty(IntEnum)` ‚Äî Easy/Medium/Hard/Elite/Master with `label`, `points` properties
- `DiaryLocation(str, Enum)` ‚Äî 12 diary regions with `xp_reward(tier)`, `min_level(tier)` methods
- `DiaryTier(str, Enum)` ‚Äî Easy/Medium/Hard/Elite
- `EquipmentSlot(str, Enum)` ‚Äî 11 equipment slots (head, weapon, body, legs, shield, cape, hands, feet, neck, ammo, ring) with `label`, `from_label` (maps wiki `2h` to `WEAPON`)
- `CombatStyle(str, Enum)` ‚Äî 28 weapon combat styles (2h Sword, Axe, Bow, Crossbow, Slash Sword, Staff, Whip, etc.) with `from_label`
- `ShopType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî 36 shop types (General, Gem, Fishing, Magic, etc.) with `from_label` fuzzy matching
- `ActivityType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî Minigame, Random event, Forestry, Raid, Activity, Boss, Distraction and Diversion, Quest, Reward with `from_label` (falls back to Activity)
- `DialogueNodeType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî line, option, condition, action, box, select, quest_action
- `DialoguePageType(StrEnum)` ‚Äî quest, npc, event, miniquest, item, pet, scenery, skill, list, questjournal
- `DialogueEntityType(StrEnum)` ‚Äî item, npc, monster, quest, location, shop, equipment, activity
- `InstructionOp(StrEnum)` ‚Äî SPEAK, BOX, QUEST, MENU, SELECT, SWITCH, JUMP_IF, JUMP, GOTO, COND, PRED, END
- `VariableType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî varp, varbit, varc_int, varc_str with `from_label`
- `ContentCategory(str, Enum)` ‚Äî quest, skill, npc, location, item, minigame, activity with `from_label`
- `FunctionalTag(str, Enum)` ‚Äî progress, toggle, counter, ui, config, storage, timer, cosmetic with `from_label`
- `ActionTriggerType(IntEnum)` ‚Äî CLICK_OBJECT, CLICK_NPC, CLICK_ITEM, USE_ITEM_ON_OBJECT, USE_ITEM_ON_NPC, USE_ITEM_ON_ITEM, CLICK_WIDGET, WIDGET_ON_ITEM with `mask`, `label` properties
- `Facility(IntEnum)` ‚Äî Bank, Furnace, Anvil, Range, Altar, Spinning wheel, Loom with `mask`, `label` properties
- `Immunity(IntEnum)` ‚Äî Poison, Venom, Cannon, Thrall, Burn with `mask`, `label` properties
- `Spellbook(str, Enum)` ‚Äî normal, ancient, lunar, arceuus with `from_label` (maps "Standard" to NORMAL)
- `Element(str, Enum)` ‚Äî air, water, earth, fire, ice, shadow, blood, smoke with `from_label`
- `MapSquareType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî color, collision, water
- `MapLinkType(str, Enum)` ‚Äî entrance, exit, fairy_ring, charter_ship, spirit_tree, gnome_glider, canoe, teleport, minecart, ship, quetzal, walkable, npc_transport
- `MAP_LINK_ANYWHERE` ‚Äî constant `"ANYWHERE"` for teleport from_location (castable from any location)
- `ALL_SKILLS_MASK`, `ALL_REGIONS_MASK` ‚Äî bitmask constants for "all"
- `COMBAT_SKILLS_MASK` ‚Äî bitmask for Attack, Strength, Defence, Hitpoints, Ranged, Magic, Prayer
